Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-17

Seeker Minesweeper ("the App") respects your privacy.

1. Data We Process

- Wallet public key (when you connect a Solana wallet).
- On-chain transaction metadata related to your actions (for example daily
  check-ins or optional in-app SOL payments).
- Local gameplay data stored on your device, such as settings, streaks,
  hints balance, and game history.

2. Local Storage

The App stores gameplay and preference data locally on your device using local
storage mechanisms. This data is used to provide core game features and is not
uploaded to our servers by default.

3. Blockchain Data

If you use wallet and on-chain features, transaction records are written to
the Solana blockchain, which is public and immutable by design.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party infrastructure providers (for example Solana RPC
providers and ecosystem tooling) to enable network functionality.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not operate a central user database
for gameplay profiles in this version of the App.

6. Your Choices

- You can use core gameplay features without wallet interaction.
- You can disconnect wallet usage by not initiating wallet actions.
- You can clear local app data via device settings.
